FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our insurance products and services.

What types of life insurance do you offer?

We offer term, whole, universal, and final expense life policies — each designed for a different stage of life. Your advisor will recommend the best fit based on income, dependents, and long-term goals.

How does Medicare Supplemental insurance work?

Supplemental (Medigap) plans help pay for costs Original Medicare doesn't cover — like copays, coinsurance, and deductibles. Plans F, G, and N are the most popular options.

What is Virtual Access to Doctors?

A 24/7 telemedicine benefit that connects you with licensed physicians by phone or video for non-emergency care, e-prescriptions, and mental health support.

What does an accident policy cover?

Accident policies pay cash benefits when you're injured — covering ER visits, hospital stays, fractures, and lost income, on top of any health insurance you already have.

When should I consider an annuity?

Annuities make sense when you want guaranteed income in retirement, tax-deferred growth, or protection against outliving your savings. We'll model your scenarios before recommending one.

Can I bundle multiple policies for a discount?

Yes. Many carriers offer multi-policy discounts when you combine life, health, and accident coverage. Ask your advisor for a bundled quote.
