## Idea 1: Corporate Trust Architecture

**Design Movement**: Neo-Corporate Minimalism — inspired by modern fintech and insurtech companies that balance authority with approachability.

**Core Principles**:
1. Clarity above all — every element serves a functional purpose
2. Trust through structure — rigid grid systems convey reliability
3. Breathing room — generous whitespace signals confidence
4. Progressive disclosure — information revealed in digestible layers

**Color Philosophy**: 
- Primary: Progressive Blue (#2D95E5) as the anchor of trust and stability
- White (#FFFFFF) as the dominant canvas, creating openness
- Dark Navy (#1B2A4A) for authoritative text
- Soft Gray (#F5F7FA) for section differentiation
- Accent Gold (#FFB800) for CTAs and energy

**Layout Paradigm**: Horizontal band architecture — full-width sections stacked vertically with alternating backgrounds, creating clear visual chapters. Hero uses asymmetric split with content left, imagery right.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Rounded pill-shaped buttons and input fields (insurance-friendly softness)
2. Subtle card elevation with soft shadows for product tiles
3. Blue gradient overlays on hero imagery

**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth, confident transitions. Hover states that gently lift elements. Form inputs that respond with micro-animations on focus.

**Animation**: Fade-up on scroll for sections, gentle scale on hover for cards, smooth color transitions on buttons. Nothing flashy — movements that feel like a reassuring handshake.

**Typography System**: 
- Headlines: Inter Display (700/800 weight) — modern, geometric authority
- Body: Inter (400/500) — clean readability
- Accent: Slightly larger line-height for breathing room
</text>

## Idea 2: Fluid Protection Aesthetic

**Design Movement**: Organic Modernism — soft curves and flowing shapes that evoke the feeling of being wrapped in protection, like water flowing around you.

**Core Principles**:
1. Organic geometry — rounded corners, wave dividers, blob shapes
2. Layered depth — overlapping elements create dimension
3. Warm professionalism — approachable without being childish
4. Motion as metaphor — flowing animations represent continuous coverage

**Color Philosophy**:
- Primary: Sky Blue (#2D95E5) representing open sky and possibility
- Deep Blue (#0A3D6B) for grounding and depth
- Warm White (#FAFBFD) as the base
- Soft Lavender (#E8F0FE) for highlighted sections
- Coral (#FF6B4A) for urgent CTAs

**Layout Paradigm**: Flowing vertical narrative with wave-shaped section dividers. Content blocks float within curved containers. Hero section uses a large curved clip-path creating a wave effect.

**Signature Elements**:
1. SVG wave dividers between sections
2. Floating card components with generous border-radius (16-24px)
3. Gradient mesh backgrounds in hero areas

**Interaction Philosophy**: Fluid, water-like transitions. Elements that ripple on interaction. Scroll-triggered animations that flow like a stream.

**Animation**: Parallax scrolling on hero, wave animations on dividers, cards that float up with a slight bounce, button ripple effects on click.

**Typography System**:
- Headlines: Plus Jakarta Sans (700) — friendly geometric with soft terminals
- Body: DM Sans (400/500) — clean and modern
- Numbers: Tabular figures for data alignment

## Idea 3: Bold Insurance Brutalism-Lite

**Design Movement**: Soft Brutalism meets Insurance — taking the bold, unapologetic nature of brutalist design but softening it with insurance-appropriate colors and rounded elements.

**Core Principles**:
1. Bold typographic hierarchy — oversized headlines that command attention
2. Visible structure — borders and outlines instead of shadows
3. Color blocking — large areas of solid color for visual impact
4. Direct communication — no ambiguity in messaging

**Color Philosophy**:
- Electric Blue (#2D95E5) as bold accent blocks
- Pure White (#FFFFFF) for contrast and breathing
- Charcoal (#2D3436) for powerful text
- Soft Blue (#E3F2FD) for secondary backgrounds
- Yellow (#FFC107) for warning/attention elements

**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric grid with bold color blocks. Large typography anchors each section. Content arranged in unexpected but functional compositions with visible grid lines.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thick borders (2-3px) on cards and containers
2. Oversized section numbers or labels
3. Bold color block backgrounds with white text overlays

**Interaction Philosophy**: Snappy, immediate responses. No easing — things appear and disappear with purpose. Hover states that invert colors.

**Animation**: Clip-path reveals on scroll, text that types in, cards that snap into position, bold color transitions on state changes.

**Typography System**:
- Headlines: Space Grotesk (700) — geometric with character
- Body: Work Sans (400) — neutral and readable
- Accents: Monospace for data/numbers
